Age Restrictions

There are only 2 age restrictions: client’s age 21 and over; at least 2 years of driving experience. If the driver does not meet these requirements, the company may ask for higher deposit amount.

List of Required Documents

There are only 2 age restrictions: To rent a car, Ukrainian citizen needs to provide a passport and driver's license. To conclude a car rental agreement a foreigner must submit a passport and the International Driving Permit or a national driving license with the name and surname written in Latin letters.

Deposit

Besides rental payment, the client is obligated to make the deposit (excess) for the vehicle. It can be made by cash or credit card. In case the customer is using the card, the necessary amount is blocked for the period of lease. The deposit is refunded to the client, as long as the car is returned in good condition. The amount of the deposit depends on the class of the vehicle.

Means of Payment

For your convenience, we offer various payment methods, including wire transfers (plus VAT). The payment can also be made in cash or by credit card. The payment is made before the trip. The client pays the whole rental amount. If he/she wants to extend the lease, he/she will also need to make the prepayment for additional days. In the case of an early return, the payment is recalculated according to the actual time of car usage.

Number of Kilometers Driven

There are only 2 age restrictions: The daily limit is 100 km. However, the company only considers the total number of kilometers per the entire period of the lease, which means that if you took a car for 10 days, the distance you can drive during this period must not exceed 1000 km. The company also offers the "No Course Limitations" service. Our managers will be happy to provide you with more detailed information about it.
